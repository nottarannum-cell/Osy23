Our Mission

KnowYourHair is dedicated to helping you discover your hair's unique needs and embrace natural care for healthy, beautiful hair. We believe that understanding your hair's porosity, scalp type, and specific concerns is the first step toward a personalized hair care routine that works.

Our platform provides educational resources, interactive tests, and personalized recommendations to guide you on your hair care journey.

What We Offer

  • • Hair Porosity Tests: Simple tests to determine your hair's porosity level (Low, Medium, or High)
  • • Scalp Analysis: Tools to help identify your scalp type (Normal, Dry, or Oily)
  • • Personalized Routines: AI-generated hair care routines tailored to your specific needs
  • • Educational Resources: Comprehensive guides on oils, ingredients, common mistakes, and hair concerns

Important Disclaimer

We are not dermatologists. This website provides general information and educational content about hair care. It is not intended to replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

For serious scalp or hair issues, persistent hair loss, severe dandruff, or any concerning symptoms, please consult a qualified dermatologist or healthcare professional. This site provides general information only and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice.
